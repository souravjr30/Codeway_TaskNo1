About me

Computer Science Graduate | Coding Enthusiast

I am a passionate short film director and the co-founder of Paradox Productions, an unregistered startup based in the College of Engineering Chengannur. I made my debut with the acclaimed short film "Eight," which has been recognized as one of the best super short films in the 2023 Student World Impact Film Festival. Additionally, "Eight" secured first place in an online short film competition held as part of IIT Indore's Fluxus 2023 event.

I am actively engaged as a game developer specializing in the Unity engine. I am constantly seeking opportunities to enhance my skills and have recently set my sights on exploring the Unreal engine as well.

I am deeply passionate about expanding my proficiency in programming languages, with a specific focus on mastering front-end web development. My ultimate goal is to cultivate a comprehensive understanding of the tech industry.
